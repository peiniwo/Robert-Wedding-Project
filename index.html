-- Robert Chang, Wedding Singer --

While Robert was not trained as a professional musician, he has relentless love and passion for music. He appreciates how music can bring people back in time, how it can leave memorable marks during special moments in our lives, and how it can bring us together during times of calamity. With such love and passion, Robert wishes to bring joy and happiness to others through voice and music.

-- Joyce Yang, Pianist --

Scientist by training, musician at heart.

Joyce began classical piano lessons at age 6 and developed an interest in various forms of music in college as she explored jazz, gospel, and Baroque music. Although she has deviated far from the path to become a concert pianist, some of her happiest moments are still found when she sits in front of a piano as her hands dance free amongst the 88 black and white keys.
